Elote with Crushed Takis

Description

Add crushed Takis to this Mexican street food classic for an extra dose of heat.

Ingredients

  • 6 ears of corn
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup crumbled Cotija cheese
  • Juice of two limes
  • Grated lime zest
  • 1/2 Cup crushed Takis
  • Tajin seasoning

Directions

  1. Mix the mayonnaise, sour cream, cotija cheese, lime juice, and lime zest. Adjust proportions to taste.
  2. Grill corn until lightly charred.
  3. Cover grilled corn in crema mixture.
  4. Sprinkled with crushed Takis and Tajin seasoning.
